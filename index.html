INDIA

One of the world’s best-selling whiskies, Imperial Blue is a superior
blend of Indian grain spirits and imported Scotch malts. Launched
in India in 1997, its exceptional smoothness is now appreciated by
whisky connoisseurs worldwide. Since Imperial Blue became part of
Pernod Ricard in 2002, its success has grown year on year, and
today it is the group’s largest brand by volume.
